Ample

Written by Bennet Leff

The Ample Sampler is a digtal drum machine, sampler, sequencer, and sound mangler. It is not too dissimilar from the Elektron Octatrack. However, Ample makes an effort to provide extensibility and ease of use in a cheaper package. Ultimately, the Ample should be cheaper, easier to use, and more powerful than its predecessors.

This blog will mostly act as a dev blog for the Ample but may include some detours.
